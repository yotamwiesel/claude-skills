# Content Repurposer

One piece of content. Ten to fifteen outputs. Zero energy lost in translation.

The rule: every repurposed piece must feel like it was *made* for that platform — not copied and pasted from somewhere else. Platform-native or it's not worth posting.

---

## Step 1 — Understand the Source

Before repurposing anything, answer these:

1. **What format is the original?** (YouTube video / podcast / blog / long caption / script)
2. **What's the core idea?** (1 sentence — the thing that makes someone stop and care)
3. **What's the emotional hook?** (What feeling does it create — curiosity, relief, validation, urgency?)
4. **Who is the intended audience?**
5. **Which platforms are we repurposing FOR?** (Instagram / YouTube / LinkedIn / Email / Twitter/X)

If the user shares raw content without context, extract answers from the content itself. Only ask if truly unclear.

---

## Step 2 — Mine the Gold

Read through the source content and pull out the raw material:

### Extraction Checklist
- **The hook** — what's the single best opening line buried in this piece?
- **The story** — is there a personal moment, failure, or turning point?
- **The data/proof** — any specific numbers, results, timeframes?
- **The frameworks** — any 3-step processes, lists, or structures?
- **The contrasts** — any "I used to think X, but actually Y" moments?
- **The quotes** — any lines so good they could stand alone?
- **The questions** — anything the audience is silently asking throughout?

Highlight 5–8 "gold pieces" before building any outputs.

---

## Step 3 — Build the Repurpose Stack

Use this menu. Match each gold piece to the right format:

### 🎬 Instagram / TikTok / YouTube Shorts (Reels/Shorts)
| Format | Best used for |
|---|---|
| Hook-story-lesson Reel | Core story or turning point |
| Myth-bust Reel | Any "I used to think X" moment |
| 1 tip in 30 seconds | A single actionable insight |
| Reaction/commentary | A data point or trend from the piece |
| "The part nobody talks about" | The uncomfortable truth buried in the piece |

### 🖼️ Instagram Carousel
| Format | Best used for |
|---|---|
| Step-by-step carousel | Frameworks or processes |
| "X things I learned" | Lists or reflection points |
| Before/After | Any transformation narrative |
| Myth bust carousel | Contrasts and reframes |
| Quote card series | Standalone lines from the original |

### 📝 Instagram Caption (single post)
| Format | Best used for |
|---|---|
| Personal story caption | Core narrative moments |
| Bold take caption | The most opinionated line in the piece |
| Relatable moment caption | Audience pain point observations |

### 📧 Email
| Format | Best used for |
|---|---|
| Story email | Full narrative arc from the piece |
| Tip email | 1–3 actionable insights |
| Reflection email | Slower, more personal version of the content |

### 🐦 Twitter/X Thread
| Format | Best used for |
|---|---|
| "I learned X things about Y" thread | Frameworks and lists |
| Story thread | Personal narrative with clear beats |
| Controversial take thread | Bold, opinionated moments |

### 💼 LinkedIn Post
| Format | Best used for |
|---|---|
| Professional story post | Career/business angle of the piece |
| Data + insight post | Numbers and results |
| Framework breakdown | 3-step or structured thinking |

## Step 4 — Repurpose Rules (Non-Negotiable)

### Every piece must:
- **Lead with the strongest version of the hook** — not a watered-down intro
- **Feel complete** — even at 3 slides or 30 seconds, it stands alone
- **Match platform behaviour** — Reels are punchy, emails can breathe, carousels build slowly
- **Preserve the original voice** — no cleaner, no more polished, no different personality
- **Have ONE clear idea** — not a summary of the full piece

### Never:
- Copy-paste sentences directly and call it repurposing
- Include context that only makes sense if you watched/read the original
- Use the same hook for more than one output
- Lose the emotional core in the translation

---

## Step 5 — Output Format

Deliver repurposed content in this structure:

```
SOURCE SUMMARY
Core idea: [1 sentence]
Emotional hook: [the feeling it creates]
Gold pieces identified: [list of 5–8]

---

OUTPUT 1 — [Format] for [Platform]
[Full content]

OUTPUT 2 — [Format] for [Platform]
[Full content]

... (continue through 10–15 outputs)

---

POSTING ORDER SUGGESTION
[Which pieces to post first, second, third — and why]
```

---

## Pacing Note

Don't post everything at once. Suggest a 2–3 week drip schedule from one source piece. This makes one YouTube video feel like a full month of content.

Remind the user: "The original video / post is the iceberg. Everything you just got is just the visible tip — and it's enough to run for weeks."

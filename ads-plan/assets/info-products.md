# Info Products & Courses Paid Advertising Template

## Industry Characteristics

- Impulse-buy potential for low-ticket ($7-$97) products
- Webinar/VSL funnels dominate for high-ticket ($500-$5,000+)
- Creator/founder personal brand is the biggest asset
- Emotional decision making; transformation promises drive sales
- High margins (80-95%) allow aggressive ad spend
- Retargeting is critical; multi-touchpoint journeys
- Seasonal peaks around New Year (resolution buying) and September (back-to-learning)

## Recommended Platform Mix

| Platform | Role | Budget % | Why |
|----------|------|----------|-----|
| Meta (FB/IG) | Primary | 35-40% | Precise interest targeting, video-heavy, retargeting |
| YouTube | Primary | 35-40% | Long-form content, VSL pre-rolls, intent-based audiences |
| Email | Supporting | 15-20% | Nurture sequences, launch campaigns, repeat buyers |
| TikTok | Testing | 5-10% | Education content, organic-to-paid amplification (min $50/day campaign) |
| Google Search | Testing | 5% | High-intent "best [topic] course" queries |

## Campaign Architecture

```
Account; Meta
├── Cold Traffic; Prospecting
│   ├── Ad Set: Interest stacks (3-5 related interests)
│   ├── Ad Set: Lookalike 1% of purchasers
│   ├── Ad Set: Lookalike 1% of email list
│   └── Ad Set: Broad (no targeting, creative-driven)
├── Warm Traffic; Lead Nurture
│   ├── Ad Set: Lead magnet downloaders (not yet purchased)
│   ├── Ad Set: Webinar registrants (not yet attended)
│   └── Ad Set: Email subscribers (no purchase)
├── Hot Traffic; Retargeting
│   ├── Ad Set: Sales page visitors (3 days)
│   ├── Ad Set: Cart abandoners
│   ├── Ad Set: Webinar attendees (didn't buy)
│   └── Ad Set: Video viewers 75%+ (7 days)
├── Buyer Campaigns
│   ├── Ad Set: Upsell to next product
│   └── Ad Set: Cross-sell related products
└── Advantage+ Shopping (if product catalog)

Account; YouTube
├── Brand
│   └── Brand terms + course name
├── Discovery
│   ├── In-stream: 2-5 min VSL-style ads (skippable)
│   ├── In-feed: educational content thumbnails
│   └── Shorts: hook → value → CTA
├── Custom Intent
│   ├── Competitor course names
│   ├── "[Topic] course" queries
│   └── "[Topic] tutorial" queries
└── Retargeting
    └── Channel viewers, website visitors
```

## Creative Strategy

### What Works for Info Products
- **Founder-to-camera video**: authentic, personal, story-driven
- **Student testimonials**: specific results with numbers ("made $10K in 30 days")
- **Free value content**: teach something valuable, then pitch the course
- **Webinar/challenge ads**: "Join my free 5-day challenge"
- **Before/after transformation**: student journey stories
- **Carousel**: key takeaways or curriculum preview
- **UGC-style**: casual, phone-recorded testimonials (TikTok/Reels)

### Funnel-Specific Creative
| Funnel Stage | Creative Type | CTA |
|-------------|--------------|-----|
| Lead magnet | Free guide/checklist ad | "Download Free" |
| Webinar | Founder video, value preview | "Save My Spot" |
| VSL/Sales page | Problem-agitation-solution video | "Watch Free Training" |
| Tripwire ($7-$27) | Quick value pitch, urgency | "Get It Now" |
| Core offer ($97-$997) | Testimonial, transformation | "Enroll Now" |
| High-ticket ($1K+) | Case study, founder credibility | "Apply Now" |

### Video Ad Structure (Meta/YouTube)
1. **Hook** (0-3s): pattern interrupt, bold claim, or question
2. **Problem** (3-15s): agitate the pain point
3. **Solution** (15-45s): introduce your framework/method
4. **Proof** (45-60s): testimonials, results, credentials
5. **CTA** (60-75s): clear next step with urgency

## Targeting Strategy

### Meta
- **Interests**: Tony Robbins, Gary Vee, Marie Forleo (or niche equivalents)
- **Behaviors**: engaged shoppers, recent online course purchasers
- **Lookalikes**: 1% of purchasers (best), 1% of email list, 1% of webinar attendees
- **Broad**: no targeting, let creative and algorithm decide (works at scale)
- **Exclusions**: existing purchasers of the same product, email unsubscribes

### YouTube
- **Custom intent**: search terms for your topic ("learn [skill]", "[topic] course")
- **Competitor channels**: target viewers of competitor YouTube channels
- **In-market**: education, online courses
- **Affinity**: lifelong learners, business professionals
- **Placements**: specific high-traffic videos in your niche

### Email List Integration (Critical)
- Upload customer lists monthly for exclusions and lookalikes
- Segment by: purchasers, webinar attendees, email opens, lead magnet downloads
- Dynamic retargeting based on funnel stage

## Common Pitfalls

- Spending big on cold traffic to sales page without a lead magnet/webinar warm-up
- Not tracking backend revenue (upsells, email sales); front-end ROAS looks terrible
- Same creative running for 60+ days (Meta fatigue sets in at 2-4 weeks)
- YouTube ads without strong hooks; skip rate kills you in the first 5 seconds
- Broad targeting too early (need 50+ conversions/week for Broad to work on Meta)
- Not testing price points before scaling ad spend
- Ignoring email nurture; ads drive leads, email closes sales
- Over-promising in ad copy; leads to refund requests and policy violations

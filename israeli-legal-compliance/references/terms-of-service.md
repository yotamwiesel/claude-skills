## Required Sections

### 1. פרטי החברה

- Company name, Israeli company ID (ח.פ.), address
- Contact email: support@{domain}
- Website URL

### 2. תיאור השירות

**Must be comprehensive — list ALL features.** Example feature categories:

- **פורום קהילתי:** Posts, comments, likes, mentions, file uploads, categories
- **קורסים ולמידה:** Online courses with modules, video lessons, downloadable materials, progress tracking
- **אירועים:** Online/in-person events, RSVPs, Zoom/Google Meet integration
- **סקרים:** Polls within community
- **מבחנים:** Online quizzes with multiple-choice, passing scores, result tracking
- **הודעות פרטיות:** Direct messaging between users
- **גיימיפיקציה:** Points, levels, badges, streaks, leaderboard (see dedicated section)
- **פרופיל משתמש:** Public profile with activity, badges, completed courses, activity heatmap
- **התראות בזמן אמת:** Real-time notifications for activity

### 3. הרשמה וחשבון משתמש

- Accurate info required
- User responsible for password security
- No account sharing
- Report unauthorized use immediately
- Minimum age: 16. Under 18: parental consent required.

### 4. שימוש מותר

Standard prohibitions plus feature-specific rules:
- No offensive, threatening, misleading, or illegal content
- No spam, phishing, malware
- No unauthorized access to data/systems
- No service disruption
- **No DM abuse** (marketing, harassment, spam via direct messages)
- **No gamification abuse** (multiple accounts for point farming, etc.)

### 5. מערכת גיימיפיקציה (if applicable)

**Critical legal clause: non-redeemable.**

- Points earned for activity (posts, comments, lesson completions, daily activity, streaks)
- Levels based on accumulated points
- Badges for specific achievements
- Streaks tracking consecutive activity days
- Leaderboard rankings (weekly, monthly, all-time)

### 9. ביטול עסקה והחזרים

**Must include all of these sub-sections:**

#### זכות ביטול עסקת מכר מרחוק
- 14 days from purchase/confirmation (whichever later)
- Min 7 days before service starts
- Fee: 5% or 100 NIS (lower)

#### זכות ביטול — תוכן דיגיטלי
- Digital content = "information" per Computer Law 5755-1995
- Section 14g(d): **no refund after access** (viewing, downloading, using)
- Can cancel within 14 days IF no access yet

#### ביטול מנוי מתמשך
- Cancel anytime via website (Settings > Subscriptions), email, phone, or oral
- Effective in 3 business days (6 if registered mail)
- No refund for already-paid period with access
- Written confirmation (email) on cancellation

#### אוכלוסיות מיוחדות
- Seniors (65+), disabled, new immigrants (5 years): **4-month cancellation window**
- Same 5%/100 NIS fee

Link to full cancellation policy page.

- Service provided AS-IS
- No liability for indirect/consequential damages

- Right to suspend/close accounts violating terms
- **Consumer law override:** nothing in terms overrides mandatory consumer protection rights

### 12. תקשורת שיווקית (דבר פרסומת)

Section 30a Communications Law compliance:
- Marketing only with **explicit prior consent**
- Must label with "פרסומת" in subject line + advertiser details
- **Free unsubscribe link** in every marketing message
- System messages (registration confirmation, password reset, activity alerts) are NOT marketing — no separate consent needed

### 13. סיום שימוש

- User can close account anytime via settings
- Platform can terminate/restrict for violations (with or without notice)
- On closure: gamification data (points, badges, streaks) deleted

### 14. דין חל וסמכות שיפוט

- Israeli law
- Exclusive jurisdiction: Tel Aviv district courts
- Standard contract per Contracts (General Part) Law 5743-1982
- Mandatory law overrides any conflicting term

- Continued use = consent

## General Rules for New Images

1. **Always write in Hebrew** (unless image shows English text)
2. **Include the primary keyword** of the page
3. **Describe what's in the image** — don't just stuff keywords
4. **Keep under 125 characters** for best accessibility
5. **Never use** `alt=""` or skip the alt attribute
6. **For decorative images** (borders, spacers): use `alt=""` intentionally
